Watermelon Lemonade Slush

A delicious and regreshing watermelon drink you are going to love

Ingredients

  • 1 cup seeded watermelon
  • 1 1/4 cups of water
  • 3 tablespoons of sweetened strawberry lemonade mix

Steps

  1. Freeze the watermelon pieces until solid
  2. Place the frozen watermelon, water and strawberry lemonade mix in a blender, blend until slushy
